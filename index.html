About

In the past few years, Taiwan has been going through many food safety crises. Products like packaged drinks, oil, instant noodles and milk were once reported containing illegally-added chmicals which could did harm to our health. Which made it so sarcastic was that some products were in our daily meal. We ate them everyday for more than a decade. Even the leading food brands lost people's trust.

Which food can we trust? These products should not go into our mouths. We don't want to eat them. But how can we do to avoid eating them. How do we know the food in my mouth is safe or not. In this case, we came up with the idea - OpenEats!


Our team includes people specializing in different areas. We have PMs, businessmen, developers and designers. We are a cool team and paossionate in Openeats project. And with no doubt, we are the people who would never put those unsafe food in the mouth.
